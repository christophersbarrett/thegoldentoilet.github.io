Presenting at the Web-5 conference in Beziers, France April 4-6th

I'll be presenting at the Web-5 Conference in Béziers, France, April 4th through the 6th!

Web-5 is a new conference focusing on JavaScript, Html5, CSS3, and WebGL. There are a great variety of speakers, including my current CEO and Dojo Toolkit Founder Dylan Schiemann.

I will be giving two presentations: one on dgrid, the new grid project in the Dojo Foundation to which I am a contributor and eventd which is a unit-testing tool written 100% in JavaScript and currently plays well Dojo's testing framework DOH. Needless to say, I'll be blogging on these two subjects in the future.

I'm looking forward to meeting some great enthusiasts as well as learning about some new technology that I haven't had a chance to play with yet.
